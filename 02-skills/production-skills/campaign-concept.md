## CAMPAIGN DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1. Every campaign ladders to: "Cuemath makes kids MathFit for the AI world"
2. NRI channel performance: referral 41.6%, organic brand 34%, Google brand 28.4%, Meta 15.1%
3. Non-NRI channel performance: Meta 0.6% — essentially non-converting via ads
4. Content mix: 4 Foundation Rebuilder, 3 Personalization Seeker, 2 Confidence Builder, 1 universal per 10 pieces
5. Lead with emotion -> Anchor to system -> Close on outcome
6. The Tutor is the emotional anchor (60.8% mention tutor). Brand builds around the system.
7. Telugu testimonials are highest-performing but most underrepresented — flag this gap
8. Accelerator segment = highest churn risk. Flag in every campaign that targets this segment.
9. Math-anchored hooks convert 3-5x better than lifestyle hooks on QL->TD%

## BRAND RULES

- MathFit(TM) on first mention. FUAR order.
- Parent-facing always. Enrichment, not remediation.
- No banned words: classroom, center, guaranteed marks, shortcuts, easy math, etc.
- Outcomes over feelings. Specific over abstract.
- Voice: Calm, confident, intelligent. The Guru-Coach.
