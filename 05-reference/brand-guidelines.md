# CUEMATH MATHFIT™ BRAND GUIDELINES

## Brand Essence & Promise
- **Core Promise:** A personalized math ecosystem building deep understanding, confidence, and real-world problem-solving—for school, competitions, and life.
- **Tagline:** "Making Kids MathFit™"
- **Brand Essence:** "The MathFit™ child doesn't just excel at math. They excel at thinking."

## Tone of Voice
- **Aspirational & Forward-Thinking:** Frame math as a lifelong skill
- **Confident Yet Compassionate:** Authority without superiority
- **Empathetic & Human-Centered:** Connect with parental concerns
- **Scientifically Credible, Not Jargony:** Proven terms with relatable examples
- **Style:** Simple, warm, intelligent, conversational — like speaking to a smart friend

## DO's
- Sound calm, confident, intelligent
- Speak to parents as thinking adults
- Feel long-term and trustworthy
- Make MathFit™ feel obvious and aspirational
- Show thinking transformations (not vanity wins)
- Use the "thinking vs. calculating" contrast frequently
- Ladder all ads to: "Cuemath will make my child MathFit™ for the AI world"
- Use ™ on first prominent mention and section headers
- Frame tutors as "coaches who specialize in your child's thinking"
- Anchor messaging in outcomes, testimonials, retention data
- Make math meaningful, not easy

## DON'Ts
- Promise shortcuts
- Hype speed or tricks
- Say "guaranteed marks"
- Over-index on ease or fun
- Use childish visuals, loud colors, gimmicky stock imagery
- Compete in "math learning" as a commodity category
- Sound remedial or transactional
- **Don't Say:** "Math is easy" → **Say:** "Math makes sense when taught the right way"

## 4 USPs
1. **Handpicked Tutors:** Top 1%, trained in pedagogy & child psychology
2. **Personalized Ecosystem:** Customized for current level, goals, ambitions
3. **The Cuemath™ Way:** "Cue don't tell" — deep conceptual understanding
4. **Custom-Built Platform:** Collaborative smart whiteboard, gamified engagement

## MathFit™ Definition
Ability to reason clearly, see patterns, explain ideas, solve unfamiliar problems with confidence.
- **IS:** Reasoning, flexible, durable, deep understanding, future-proof
- **IS NOT:** Speed tricks, worksheet completion, formula memorization

## Master Frame
"AI calculates. Humans must think."

## Transformation Narrative (Before → After)
- Math anxiety → Math confidence
- Passive calculation → Active reasoning
- Fragile memorization → Deep understanding
- School struggle → Confident problem-solving
- Average performance → Outlier thinking

## Visual Guidelines
- Clean, calm, thoughtful aesthetic
- Show struggle, thinking aloud, collaboration — not just smiling completion
- No childish exaggeration, loud colors, or gimmicky stock visuals

## Messaging Hierarchy
1. **Lead:** Tutor + system together; child's emotional transformation
2. **Support:** Understanding and confidence as MathFit outcomes
3. **Reinforce:** Personalization and school alignment
4. **Never lead:** AI, technology, worksheet volume, speed, grade guarantees

## Proof Formats (Ranked)
1. Parent transformation stories
2. Named tutor-within-system testimonials
3. Multi-year/sibling stories
4. Child's voice testimonials
5. MathFit philosophy content
- **Avoid:** Statistics alone, app-store ratings, AI features, offer-heavy content

## Trademark Usage
- Always MathFit™ (with ™) on first mention, headlines, hero sections, ads
- After first mention: ™ may be omitted in body copy for readability
- Must reappear in section headers, end frames, logos
- **Correct:** MathFit™ | **Incorrect:** MathFit, mathfit, Math Fit
