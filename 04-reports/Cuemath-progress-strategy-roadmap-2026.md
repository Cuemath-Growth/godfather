## 4. Brand & Positioning Strategy

### 4.1 The Core Problem

Cuemath has a **categorization problem**, not an awareness problem. NRI parents already know Cuemath — but they bucket it alongside Kumon, Mathnasium, and local tutors under generic "math tutoring." The job is to move Cuemath off that list entirely.

### 4.2 The One Belief

> **"Cuemath doesn't teach math. They teach kids how to think."**

### 4.3 The Master Frame

> **"AI calculates. Humans must think."**

### 4.4 The MathFit™ Framework (FUAR)

| Letter | Pillar | What It Means |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| **F** | Fluency | Speed and accuracy with number operations |
| **U** | Understanding | The "why" behind the method |
| **A** | Application | Using math to solve real-world and novel problems |
| **R** | Reasoning | Logical thinking under uncertainty |

**Mindset Dimension:** Interest · Confidence · Growth Mindset & Resilience · Math Relevance

MathFit™ is the proprietary category Cuemath is building — not a product feature, not a tagline. A state of mathematical readiness that only Cuemath occupies.

### 4.5 The Cuemath Way — How We Teach

- **"Cue, Don't Tell"** — Tutors ask questions so the child discovers answers. Builds thinking, not dependency.
- **Productive Struggle** — Problems just hard enough to stretch thinking. Struggle is the path.
- **High Talk Ratio (Talk-o-Meter)** — The child does the talking and explaining. Cuemath measures it.
- **Interleaved Learning** — Mixes problem types to build flexible pattern recognition.

All neuroscience-backed: productive struggle, interleaving, retrieval practice, explanation, teaching back.

### 4.6 The 5 USPs

| # | USP | What It Means for the Parent |
|---|-----|------------------------------|
| 1 | **Handpicked Tutors** | Top 1% certified, trained in math & child psychology. "Coaches who specialize in *your* child's thinking." |
| 2 | **One Personalized Program** | From school success to future readiness — all in one plan. |
| 3 | **The Cuemath Way** | Deep conceptual understanding that stays for life. |
| 4 | **Custom-Built Platform** | Collaborative smart whiteboard, gamified engagement. |
| 5 | **Proven Outcomes** | 4.9 Trustpilot, 200K students. "Confidence first, grades follow." |

### 4.7 Competitive Positioning

| Dimension | Cuemath | Kumon | Mathnasium | Private Tutors | Online Platforms |
|-----------|---------|-------|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Method** | "Cue Don't Tell" — guided discovery | Worksheet repetition | Center-based instruction | Homework help | Self-paced video/app |
| **Personalization** | 1:1 tutor + adaptive platform + custom curriculum | One-size-fits-all | Small group | Varies | Algorithm-driven |
| **What's measured** | FUAR (thinking fitness) | Speed & accuracy | Grade-level | Test scores | Completion rate |
| **Delivery** | 100% online, global | Physical centers | Physical centers | In-person or online | App/website |
| **Moat** | MathFit™ + pedagogy + platform | Scale + brand | Local presence | Relationship | Content library |

### 4.8 Messaging Hierarchy

1. **Lead with:** Tutor + system together; child's emotional transformation
2. **Support with:** Understanding and confidence as MathFit™ outcomes
3. **Reinforce:** Personalization and school alignment
4. **Never lead with:** AI, technology, worksheet volume, speed, or grade guarantees

### 4.9 Brand Voice

The Mentor — wise, patient, clear, confident without pressure. Simple, warm, intelligent. Like a smart friend who happens to be an expert.

### 4.10 Copy Atoms

## 8. Non-Indian Asian Expansion — The Next Growth Vector

*Source: 32 in-depth interviews with US Non-Indian Asian parents (Chinese, Korean, Vietnamese, Filipino). This is the integrated GTM strategy for expanding beyond NRI into a much larger US addressable market.*

### 8.1 The Paradigm Shift

**The "Deficit Model":** Non-Indian Asian parents view US public school math as fundamentally broken — "2 years behind." Enrichment isn't optional; it's a strategic defensive measure. The baseline for "normal" is the community's accelerated standard, not the school district's.

**"Chi Ku" (Eating Bitterness):** Parents value productive struggle and discipline. They distrust programs that are "too easy" or "just playing games." They want rigor without the soul-crushing trauma of legacy programs. **Cuemath's pedagogy is a natural fit** — productive struggle is literally our method.

### 8.2 The Strategic Wedge: "Kumon Rehab"

The market is polarized between "Drill and Kill" (Kumon) and "Elite Competition" (AoPS). Our primary acquisition attack vector: **target the massive volume of parents experiencing "Kumon fatigue."**

> "Keep the discipline, ditch the drudgery."

We offer a "Balanced Pragmatist" solution: keep the intense cognitive discipline and routine they demand, but replace rote repetition with deep conceptual thinking and human-led engagement.

### 8.3 The Acquisition Challenge

**Brand awareness is zero** in this segment (32/32 parents had not heard of Cuemath). Discovery operates on a strict validation sequence:

1. **Private Seeding (The "Coupon Loop"):** Discovery happens in closed WeChat groups where "Alpha Parents" share monetized referral codes.
2. **Public Validation (The "Seriousness Filter"):** Parents immediately vet seeded names on public forums (Wenxuecity, Reddit) for comparative reasoning and "proof of rigor."

**Operational ask:** We must build a double-sided WeChat-compatible referral program and publish **un-gated, highly granular curriculum maps (PDFs)**. If our curriculum is a "black box," it cannot be peer-reviewed by the community, and we will not be shortlisted.

### 8.4 The "Seriousness Filter"

Before reading details, these parents ask: *"Is this a serious math track?"* Signals they require:

- **Tutor credentials** — generic tutors are rejected instantly. They demand credentialed experts (PhDs, former professors). This is the "Specific Teacher Cult."
- **Structured progression** — visible pathway map, not vague promises
- **Progress measurement** — the diagnostic must continue post-payment with scheduled reassessments
- **The "WeChat Brag Test"** — progress must map to Relative Superiority (Grade Level +2, percentile, competition readiness) so parents can socially defend the choice

### 8.5 Execution Segments (Layered Under Existing ICP)

These do NOT replace the 4 ICP segments — they sit underneath them for non-Indian Asian parents:

| Execution Segment | Primary ICP Need-State | Confidence | The Play |
|-------------------|----------------------|------------|----------|
| **SEG2 — Competitive/Advanced** | Accelerator | High (n=8) | "Seriousness Umbrella" — prove elite reasoning depth vs AoPS benchmark |
| **SEG1 — Catch-up/Insurance** | Foundation Rebuilder | High (n=8) | "Kumon Rehab" — discipline without drudgery, rescue from Kumon fatigue |
| **SEG3 — Learning Centers** | Foundation + Personalization | Medium (n=5) | Center-level seriousness, online — replicate assessment→plan→accountability |
| **SEG5 — App Graduates** | Personalization + Foundation | Medium (n=5) | Beyond DIY apps — human-led engagement vs gamified streaks |
| **SEG4 — Private Tutor** | Personalization | Low (n=3) | Validation-first — match tutor quality + continuity guarantee |
| **SEG6 — HS Confidence** | Confidence Builder | Low (n=3) | Validation-first — teen's judgment is decisive |

### 8.6 The 4 Growth Bets (with Kill-Switches)

| Bet | Segment | Hypothesis | Kill-Switch |
|-----|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Bet 1 (Flagship)** | SEG2 Competitive | Prove non-routine reasoning depth; progress maps to Olympiad readiness | Trial-to-paid doesn't lift after N=30 |
| **Bet 2 (Scale)** | SEG1 Catch-up | "Kumon Rehab" — leverage elite credibility to rescue from Kumon burnout | "Too easy" perception in >25% of trials |
| **Bet 3 (Scale)** | SEG3 Centers | Replicate center assessment→plan→accountability flow online | Online perceived as less serious at meaningful rates |
| **Bet 4 (Scale)** | SEG5 Apps | Human-led engagement > gamified apps for conceptual depth | Experience rated "same as app" by parents |

**Wave 0 (pre-launch):** Ship un-gated curriculum PDFs, "Master Class" tutor profiles, explicit placement assessments, "Exit Safety" policies, teacher-comment dashboards. **We cannot launch Wave 1 until the product matches the cultural expectations.**

### 8.7 Conversion & Retention Requirements

- **Trial as "Assessment Closer":** The diagnostic must explain *why* a child is placed at a specific level, highlighting the gap to Grade Level +2. A diagnostic that says "at grade level" is a failure.
- **"Exit Safety":** Transparent pricing and clear refund windows lower the fear of lock-in.
- **Day 0–30 Onboarding Journey:** Welcome + plan → Day 3-4 check-in → Day 7-14 first Progress Snapshot → Week 4 reassessment. Eliminates post-pay silence.
- **Progress Snapshots for "WeChat Bragging":** Must map to Relative Superiority (Grade Level Equivalence, percentile, competition readiness) — not just "concepts mastered."
